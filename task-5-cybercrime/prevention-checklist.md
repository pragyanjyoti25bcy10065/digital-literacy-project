## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never click on suspicious links in emails or messages
- Always verify website URLs before entering credentials
- Do not share OTP, passwords, or banking details
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
- Install antivirus or security software

### UPI Safety Tips
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Always verify receiver details before sending money
