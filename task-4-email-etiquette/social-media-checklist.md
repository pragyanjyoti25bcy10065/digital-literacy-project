## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

### ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful profile
- Think before posting content
- Use privacy settings appropriately
- Share useful and informative content
- Respect others’ opinions online

### ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread misinformation
- Avoid engaging in online arguments
- Do not overshare private details
